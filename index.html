Game Rules

Objective

The objective of this game is to defeat your opponent by achieving the highest score.

How to Play

  • Each player takes turns making moves
  • Points are awarded for successful actions
  • The game ends when a player reaches the target score
  • The player with the highest score wins

Scoring

  • Basic move: 10 points
  • Special move: 25 points
  • Bonus action: 50 points

Tips & Strategies

  • Plan your moves ahead of time
  • Watch your opponent's patterns
  • Use special moves strategically
  • Control the center of the board when possible
